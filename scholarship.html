The Government of India offers a wide range of scholarships to support needy students who aspire to pursue diverse careers after completing their 12th grade. These scholarships provide financial assistance to students from various backgrounds, enabling them to overcome economic barriers and achieve their academic goals. By providing these scholarships, the government aims to create a more equitable and inclusive educational landscape, empowering students to reach their full potential and contribute meaningfully to society.
