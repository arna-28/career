Science

Science is the systematic study of the natural world, aimed at understanding the fundamental principles governing the universe. It involves observation, experimentation, and the formulation of theories to explain natural phenomena. Science is divided into various disciplines, including physics, chemistry, biology, earth science, and social sciences, each focusing on different aspects of the world around us. Through scientific inquiry, humans have unlocked the mysteries of matter, energy, life, and the cosmos, leading to technological advancements, medical breakthroughs, and a deeper understanding of our environment. Science continually evolves, pushing the boundaries of knowledge and improving our quality of life.

Physics

Physics explores the nature of matter and energy, focusing on fundamental forces like gravity and electromagnetism. It spans classical mechanics to modern areas like quantum physics and relativity.
