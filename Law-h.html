Law

Law is a system of rules and principles established by a society or government to regulate behavior, maintain order, and ensure justice. It encompasses a wide range of areas, including criminal law, civil law, corporate law, constitutional law, and more. Laws serve to protect individual rights, resolve disputes, and uphold social order by defining what is permissible and prescribing penalties for violations. The legal system is vital in shaping society, promoting fairness, and safeguarding human rights, while continually evolving to address new social, economic, and technological challenges.

Criminal law

Criminal law deals with prosecuting and defending individuals accused of crimes such as theft, assault, or murder. It ensures justice by maintaining social order and punishing offenders.
