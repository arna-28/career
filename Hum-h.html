Humanties

Humanities is a field that explores human culture and experiences through various disciplines, including History, Literature, Philosophy, Linguistics, Art History, and Cultural Studies. It examines past events, literary works, philosophical ideas, languages, artistic expressions, and cultural phenomena to understand human behavior, societal changes, and cultural values. By analyzing these aspects, the humanities provide deep insights into the complexities of human life and the development of societies.

History explores past events, societies, and civilizations to understand their impact on the present and future.
